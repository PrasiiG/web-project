3d Project

            3D MODELING PROJECT STATEMENT                                                           My design is a cactus character sitting in a pot with a face, spikes, and two arms. It looks cute and happy, which is why I chose to make it. I was inspired by something I saw on Pinterest that looked cute and happy, and it motivated me to stay happy and positive in my life. The intended use is a 3D print, so I can place it on my office desk. Work can be stressful and tiring, but seeing this structure on my desk would make me feel more relieved and happier.                                                                                               I chose spheres for the cactus body because I wanted it to look cute, cylinders for the pot because that is what a pot looks like in real life, and cones for the spikes because they give more of a spiky look than any other shape. I used all the available tools to make the shapes fit together, which took a lot of time and effort, and I scaled shapes up and down as needed. I also used hole shapes to carve out the eyes of the smiley face and to create the hollow interior and drainage holes. The most challenging part was placing all the spikes in the right position, and I made them small, duplicated them 30 to 35 times, and placed them all around the cactus.
