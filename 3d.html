3d Project

My 3D project is a cute cactus character sitting in a pot, complete with a smiley face, spikes, and two arms. I was inspired by something I saw on Pinterest that looked happy and positive, and I wanted to create something I could 3D print and place on my desk to bring a little joy during stressful days.

I used spheres for the cactus body, cylinders for the pot, and cones for the spikes. I also used hole shapes to carve out the eyes and create drainage holes at the bottom of the pot. The most challenging part was placing all the spikes — I duplicated them 30 to 35 times and carefully positioned each one around the cactus body.Software used:Tinkercad
