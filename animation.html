Animation Project

For this project I animated the feeling of anxiety. A blue circle in the center represents a person — it starts calm, but as the background darkens, shapes begin flying in from all sides, closing in with fast movement and chaos to capture what anxiety truly feels like.

The most challenging part was creating the shaking effect, which required placing many keyframes very close together. I used all four transform properties: position, rotation, scale, and opacity. Software used: Adobe Illustrator and Adobe After Effects.
